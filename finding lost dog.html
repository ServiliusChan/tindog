What To Do If Your Dog Is Missing

Sometimes, it’s understandable that dogs are just essentially carefree. Their happiness is simple: running everywhere, chasing fast-moving objects(such as balls), and following mysterious scents. While such behaviors are typical for a dog, they can be troublesome sometimes. Many dogs are missed during their unstoppable runs. Tindog users, here’s our solution. We offer a finding dogs service for you to find your dogs when they disappear from your sight, just in case. Uploaded information about the missing dogs’ names, photos, and any related description will be notified to all users in your reported missing spots. Once our furry friends have been located by other users, they can contact our Tindog officers just to help you to get back your family members as soon as possible. This way, we hope to offer one extra solution when you feel truly desperate.
