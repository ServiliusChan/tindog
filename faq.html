Frequently Asked Qusetions Any restrictions for users?

Users who post content that may involve inappropriate or even illegal content, including but not limited to violence, nudity, child pornography, etc will be under review. It is also a process that is triggered by reports from other users. Violations of Terms of Use, as well as Community Guidelines, stand as the ultimate reference for Tindog to decide whether your account is still welcomed or not. Once we find any behaviors that have violated the above terms, appropriate actions will be taken, which may include a permanent ban, meaning that your IP address and the accounts you own will not be able to access anymore.

Please visit Community Guidelines and Terms of Use to understand what is allowed and what is prohibited.
